For Students

IIT Madras Placement Journey — a 7-part series written in 2023

If you're an MS or MTech student at IIT Madras (or any research institution) preparing for campus placements, this section is for you.

In 2023, I went through the IIT Madras placement process as an MS Research scholar — from deciding to sit for placements, to preparing for data science and analytics roles, to Day 0 interviews. I documented the entire journey in real time across seven posts.

My background was mechanical engineering and research (no CS, no prior coding). I targeted data science and quant roles during what turned out to be the biggest tech recession the world had faced. On December 1st, 2022, I received offers from Bajaj, JP Morgan & Chase, and Morgan Stanley. I joined Morgan Stanley.

I'm sharing the full series because I would have found it useful to read about someone going through exactly the same thing. Everything below is honest — the setbacks, the uncertainty, and what actually worked.

Where I am now (June 2026): Senior Associate, Trading Risk Management at Morgan Stanley, covering commodity derivatives — oil, EU power and gas, LME metals, and agricultural markets. The preparation was worth it. If you have specific questions about the placement process or about making the transition from research to finance, feel free to reach out on LinkedIn.
