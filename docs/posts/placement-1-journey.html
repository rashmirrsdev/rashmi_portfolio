Placement Journey

1 — My Placement Journey: Introduction

Rashmi Rama Sushil · January 2023 · 3 min read

In July 2020, I started my journey as an MS Research Scholar in the Department of Engineering Design at IIT Madras. During my time there, I had a wonderful experience living the life of a Master's student — different from an M.Tech programme in several ways I'll write about separately. On December 1, 2022, I received job offers from three companies: Bajaj, JP Morgan & Chase, and Morgan Stanley.

After careful consideration, I accepted the offer from Morgan Stanley and started my first job in July 2023 as a Strats and Quant Researcher in the Fixed Income division.

In this series, I'm sharing the full account of my placement journey. It's written for MS/MTech students at IIT Madras (and similar institutions) who are preparing for campus placements and want to know what it actually looks like from the inside.

What this series covers

  1. My Background — education, work experience, skills, achievements, and the resume template I used
  2. Preparation Timeline and Target Companies — when I started, which companies I targeted, and how I structured the months from July to December
  3. Coding for Non-Coders — how I taught myself DSA and Python from scratch, with no CS background
  4. Ups and Downs — the challenges, rejections, and setbacks — and how I got through them
  5. A Week Before Placements — company-specific prep for Honda, JP Morgan, and Morgan Stanley
  6. Day 0 of Placements — four interviews on December 1st, 2022: what was asked and what happened

I hope that my experience is useful to you in your own placement journey. It was a hard process, and an honest account felt more valuable than a polished one.

Best of luck.
